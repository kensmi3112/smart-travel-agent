### Detailed Itinerary

**Day 1: Arrival in Seminyak**  
Arrive at Ngurah Rai Airport (DPS). Private transfer to your villa in Seminyak. Settle in, beach walk, sunset cocktails. Welcome dinner at a beachfront restaurant.

**Days 2–3: Seminyak & Beach Time**  
Surf lessons or beach club relaxation (Potato Head or Ku Dé Ta). Shopping at Seminyak Square and spa treatments. Optional cooking class.

**Days 4–7: Ubud Cultural Immersion**  
Transfer to Ubud (2-hour scenic drive). Visit Tegallalang Rice Terraces, Sacred Monkey Forest, and Ubud Art Markets. Yoga or wellness activities. Day trip to Mount Batur or a traditional village.

**Days 8–10: Nusa Dua & South Bali**  
Move to Nusa Dua for upscale beaches. Water sports, visit Uluwatu Temple and watch the Kecak dance at sunset. Optional day trip to Nusa Penida for dramatic cliffs and beaches (boat tour).

**Days 11–13: Return to Seminyak & Leisure**  
Back to Seminyak for final shopping, more spa time, and a special farewell dinner. Flexible days for massages, beach clubs, or a half-day rice field cycling tour.

**Day 14: Departure**  
Morning at leisure before private airport transfer.

### Practical Tips for Australian Travellers

- **Visa & Entry**: Australians get 30 days visa-free on arrival (extendable). Ensure passport has 6+ months validity.
- **Health & Safety**: Get travel insurance covering medical evacuation. Drink bottled water; use mosquito repellent. Pharmacies are widely available. Register with Smart Traveller.
- **Money**: ATMs are plentiful. Use cards for larger payments; carry some IDR cash. Current exchange ~AUD 1 = IDR 10,200.
- **Transport**: Use Grab app for rides. Book private drivers for day trips (~AUD 60–80/day).
- **Communication**: Buy a local SIM (Telkomsel) at the airport for cheap data.
- **Cultural Etiquette**: Dress modestly at temples (cover shoulders/knees). Remove shoes indoors. Bargain politely at markets.
- **Sustainability**: Choose eco-friendly tours and avoid single-use plastics. Support local businesses.
- **Retirement Planning Angle**: This trip is a great “test run” for longer stays. Consider extending to 1–3 months in future for true retirement living costs.
