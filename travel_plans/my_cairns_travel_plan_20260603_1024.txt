**10-Day Relaxed Cairns Itinerary – Nature & Gentle Walks**

This itinerary is designed for a slow, enjoyable pace with plenty of downtime. It focuses on easy boardwalks, scenic lookouts, rainforest strolls, and beach time rather than hikes. Base yourself in Cairns (or split 2–3 nights in Palm Cove or Port Douglas if you prefer a quieter vibe). Use a hire car or organised small-group tours for day trips.

### Day 1: Arrival & Cairns Esplanade
- Morning/afternoon arrival. Check into accommodation.
- Gentle afternoon stroll along the **Cairns Esplanade Boardwalk** (flat, paved, 2–3 km). Watch for birds and enjoy the waterfront.
- Sunset at the **Lagoon** and dinner at a casual waterfront restaurant.

### Day 2: Cairns Botanic Gardens
- Full relaxed day at the **Cairns Botanic Gardens & Flecker Gardens**.
- Easy, mostly flat paths through rainforest sections, lily ponds, and the heritage gardens. Visit the **Visitor Centre** and **Butterfly House** (air-conditioned rest stop).
- Afternoon tea at the gardens café.

### Day 3: Palm Cove Beach Day
- Short drive (20 min) to **Palm Cove**.
- Morning beach walk along the shaded esplanade (very flat).
- Relax under the trees, swim in the calm waters (stinger net in season), and enjoy a long lunch.
- Optional gentle wander through the resort gardens.

### Day 4: Skyrail & Kuranda (Rainforest Canopy)
- Take the **Skyrail Rainforest Cableway** (one-way, very gentle).
- Short, easy boardwalk at **Red Peak** and **Barron Gorge** stations.
- Explore Kuranda village at a slow pace (markets, cafes, and the **Kuranda River Walk** – flat and shaded).
- Return by scenic train or Skyrail.

### Day 5: Great Barrier Reef – Relaxed Boat Day
- Half or full-day **glass-bottom boat or pontoon tour** (choose operators with minimal snorkelling pressure).
- Spend time on the pontoon, enjoy the underwater viewing, and do very gentle snorkelling if desired.
- Return to Cairns for a quiet evening.

### Day 6: Daintree Rainforest (Day Trip)
- Small-group day tour or self-drive to **Daintree**.
- Focus on easy boardwalks: **Daintree Discovery Centre** (canopy tower with lift), **Marrdja Boardwalk**, and **Jindalba Boardwalk**.
- Lunch at Daintree Village or a riverside café. Return to Cairns by late afternoon.

### Day 7: Atherton Tablelands Waterfalls
- Day trip to the **Atherton Tablelands**.
- Gentle stops: **Millaa Millaa Falls** (short flat walk), **Zillie Falls** lookout, and **Lake Barrine** (easy 30-min circuit boardwalk around the lake).
- Plenty of stops for tea and photos.

### Day 8: Port Douglas & Four Mile Beach
- Relaxed drive to **Port Douglas** (1 hour).
- Morning stroll along **Four Mile Beach** (flat sand).
- Explore Macrossan Street slowly, then a gentle walk through **Rex Smeal Park** and the marina.
- Optional sunset at the beach.

### Day 9: Free Day – Nature at Your Pace
- Choose your favourite spot from earlier in the week or:
  - Return to **Palm Cove** for another beach day, or
  - Do the short **Mount Whitfield Conservation Park** boardwalk (very easy section near the base) if you feel like a change of scenery.
- Afternoon spa or pool time.

### Day 10: Morning Nature & Departure
- Early gentle walk at **Cairns Botanic Gardens** (if your flight is later) or another Esplanade stroll.
- Brunch before heading to the airport.

**Pace Tips**
- Build in rest days or shorter mornings as needed.
- Carry water, hat, and reef-safe sunscreen.
- Book reef and Daintree tours in advance during peak season (June–September).
