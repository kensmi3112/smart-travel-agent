**10-Day Relaxed Sydney Itinerary – Nature & Gentle Walks**

This gentle-paced itinerary focuses on Sydney’s beautiful parks, harbour foreshores, coastal paths and bushland. It’s designed for a relaxed pace with plenty of time for coffee, beach time and rest. All walks are mostly flat or with very gentle slopes.

### Day 1: Arrival & Harbour Foreshore
- Settle into your accommodation (ideally near Circular Quay or the Rocks).  
- Gentle afternoon stroll along the **Sydney Harbour foreshore** from Circular Quay to the Opera House and Mrs Macquarie’s Chair (about 3–4 km return).  
- Evening: Watch the sunset over the harbour from Mrs Macquarie’s Point.

### Day 2: Royal Botanic Garden & The Domain
- Morning walk through the **Royal Botanic Garden** (easy paths, beautiful trees and water views).  
- Continue into **The Domain** for a relaxed loop around the lawns and fig trees.  
- Afternoon: Sit by the pond or enjoy a picnic.

### Day 3: Bondi to Bronte Coastal Walk
- Easy coastal walk from **Bondi Beach to Bronte** (one-way 2.5 km, mostly flat).  
- Stop at Bronte Beach for a swim or coffee.  
- Return by bus or extend gently to Coogee if you feel energetic.

### Day 4: Centennial Park
- Full relaxed day in **Centennial Parklands**.  
- Gentle loop around the ponds and through the paperbark forests (you can do 4–6 km at your own pace).  
- Hire a bike or just enjoy the birdlife and open spaces.

### Day 5: Manly & North Head
- Ferry from Circular Quay to Manly (lovely 30-min ride).  
- Gentle walk from Manly Beach up to **North Head Sanctuary** (easy paths with stunning harbour views).  
- Afternoon swim at Manly Beach or Shelly Beach.

### Day 6: Watsons Bay & South Head
- Ferry to Watsons Bay.  
- Easy walk along the harbour edge to **South Head** and Hornby Lighthouse (flat paths, beautiful views).  
- Lunch at a waterfront café then gentle return via the cliff-top path.

### Day 7: Ku-ring-gai Chase National Park (Day Trip)
- Take a train + bus or organised transfer to **West Head** lookout area.  
- Very gentle walks along the fire trails and short lookouts with views over Pittwater (choose 2–3 short easy tracks).  
- Picnic lunch with water views.

### Day 8: Lavender Bay & Kirribilli
- Relaxed morning ferry to Lavender Bay.  
- Gentle walk along the **harbour foreshore** to Wendy’s Secret Garden and Kirribilli.  
- Afternoon: Explore the quiet streets and enjoy café time.

### Day 9: Blue Mountains – Leura (Easy Day Trip)
- Train to Leura (about 2 hours).  
- Very gentle walks: **Leura Cascades** and **Gordon Falls** (short, mostly flat paths).  
- Stroll through Leura village and the beautiful gardens. Return by train in the afternoon.

### Day 10: Hyde Park & Farewell
- Morning wander through **Hyde Park** and the ANZAC Memorial.  
- Gentle walk down to the harbour again or revisit a favourite spot.  
- Afternoon: Relaxed lunch and departure.

**Practical Tips for Australians**
- Use an Opal card for ferries and trains – ferries are especially pleasant.  
- Best times for walks: early morning or late afternoon to avoid heat.  
- Carry water and a hat – Sydney sun is strong even in cooler months.  
- Many of these walks have excellent nearby cafés for regular breaks.
