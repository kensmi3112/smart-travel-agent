**10-Day Relaxed Gold Coast Itinerary – Nature & Gentle Walks**

This itinerary is designed for a slow, restorative pace with morning gentle walks, beach time, and plenty of downtime. It’s perfect for nature lovers who prefer easy trails over hikes. Base yourselves in **Broadbeach** or **Burleigh Heads** for easy access to everything (both have lovely beachfront cafes and parks).

### Day 1: Arrival & Burleigh Beach Settle-In
- Gentle arrival stroll along **Burleigh Beach** and the rock pool at low tide.
- Easy 30–40 min oceanfront walk to **Burleigh Head National Park** lookout (flat, well-maintained path with beautiful views).
- Afternoon: Relax on the beach or at a beachfront café.

### Day 2: Tallebudgera Creek & Palm Beach
- Morning: Flat, shaded walk along **Tallebudgera Creek** (start at the footbridge near Palm Beach). Very peaceful with birdlife.
- Afternoon: Gentle beach walk from Palm Beach to Tallebudgera or just relax by the creek.

### Day 3: Springbrook National Park (Easy Day Trip)
- Drive ~45 mins inland. Focus on the **Purling Brook Falls** circuit (the lower lookout section is very gentle) or the easy **Warringa Pool** track.
- Picnic lunch by the creek, then a short stop at **Best of All Lookout** (short, mostly flat path through ancient forest).
- Return by mid-afternoon for a relaxed evening.

### Day 4: Mount Tamborine (Rainforest & Views)
- Another relaxed hinterland day (~50 mins drive).
- Gentle walks: **Cedar Creek Falls** (easy track) and the short **Tamborine Rainforest Walk**.
- Visit the **Gallery Walk** for a slow wander and lunch, then return via scenic roads.

### Day 5: Currumbin & Elephant Rock
- Morning: Easy walk around **Currumbin Rock Pools** and along the creek.
- Afternoon: Flat coastal path from Currumbin to Elephant Rock and beyond — lovely ocean views with almost no elevation.

### Day 6: Hinze Dam & Numinbah Valley
- Scenic drive with a very gentle lakeside walk at **Hinze Dam** (flat paths with picnic areas).
- Stop at **Springbrook Road** lookouts for photos, then a relaxed lunch in the valley.

### Day 7: Coolangatta to Kirra Beach Walk
- Full relaxed beach day. Walk the beautiful flat path from **Coolangatta** through Kirra to Tugun (or just do sections).
- Plenty of spots to stop for coffee or a swim. Very easy and scenic.

### Day 8: Lamington National Park (Very Gentle Option)
- Drive to **Green Mountains** section. Stick to the short, flat **Tree Top Walk** area and easy rainforest boardwalks near the visitor centre.
- Picnic and enjoy the birds. Keep the day short and return for a beach sunset.

### Day 9: Broadbeach & Hinterland Markets
- Morning gentle walk along **Broadbeach** foreshore.
- If it’s a weekend, visit the **Carrara Markets** or **Tamborine Mountain markets** for a slow wander (very relaxed pace).
- Afternoon beach or pool time.

### Day 10: Final Gentle Morning & Departure
- Last easy walk at **Burleigh Head** or along **Tallebudgera Creek** again.
- Brunch overlooking the ocean before you head home.

**Tips for a relaxed trip:**
- Rent a car — it makes the hinterland days much easier and more enjoyable.
- Best walking times: early morning (cooler and quieter).
- Pack: light layers, hat, water, and reef-safe sunscreen.
- Many of these walks have excellent cafes nearby so you can take it slow.
